FAQ

Common questions about AI

Do I need to know how to write AI prompts?
No. Just describe your goal in plain language — "Send a re-engagement campaign to inactive subscribers" — and the AI handles the rest. No prompt engineering required.
Can I edit what the AI creates?
Absolutely. Every AI-generated campaign opens in the drag-and-drop editor so you can tweak copy, swap images, or adjust layout before sending.
Is my data used to train AI models?
No. Your campaign data, contacts, and engagement history are used only to power your own AI features. They are never shared with or used to train any third-party or shared model.
Is AI available on all plans?
The AI Campaign Builder is available on all plans including Starter. The AI Research Agent and advanced AI optimization features are available on Standard and above.
